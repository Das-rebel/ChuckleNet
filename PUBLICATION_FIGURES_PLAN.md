## 🎨 **FIGURE GENERATION STRATEGY**

### **Design Principles**
1. **Publication Quality**: 300 DPI minimum resolution
2. **Color Accessibility**: Color-blind friendly palettes
3. **Font Consistency**: Match venue template requirements
4. **Sizing Compliance**: Follow ACL/COLING formatting guidelines
5. **Visual Clarity**: High contrast for black-and-white printing

### **Technical Implementation**
- **Primary Tool**: Python matplotlib/seaborn for data visualization
- **Architecture Diagrams**: Lucidchart or draw.io for system designs
- **Image Processing**: Adobe Illustrator or Inkscape for final polish
- **File Formats**: PDF (vector), PNG (raster backup), EPS (publication)

### **Color Schemes**
**ACL/EMNLP Theme**:
- Primary: Deep Blue (#0066CC)
- Secondary: Forest Green (#228B22)
- Accent: Burnt Orange (#CC5500)
- Background: White with light gray accents

**COLING Theme**:
- Primary: Royal Blue (#4169E1)
- Secondary: Multi-cultural colors (Red, Yellow, Green, Blue)
- Accent: Purple (#6A0DAD)
- Background: White with international flag accents
